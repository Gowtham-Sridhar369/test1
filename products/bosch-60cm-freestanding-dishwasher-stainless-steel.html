Have your dinnerware and glassware expertly washed in this Bosch Series 2 60cm Freestanding Dishwasher Stainless Steel SMS2ITI02A with its five spray levels for maximum water contact and optimum results, while Wi-Fi connectivity allows for easy management of settings via an app for convenience like never before. It has a 13-place setting with two foldable cup shelves in the upper basket with glass holders to securely hold your favourite wine glasses.

This Bosch dishwasher uses 12.7 litres of water per wash on the Eco cycle with a cold water connection, and it has a LoadSensor to ensure only a precise amount of water and electricity are utilised based on the load size. It has a 3.5 Star Energy Rating and a 4 Star WELS Water Rating, WELS Licence Number D02581. Run a load day or night thanks to its EcoSilence Drive advanced motor which works without brushes for greater energy efficiency and silent performance. Choose from five programs like 'Favourite' for downloaded programs, 'Pots & Pans' for an intensive clean, or a sensor-controlled Auto Wash based on the level of soiling. Reduce wash time with the SpeedPerfect option, select Extra Dry for additional drying, or HygienePlus for an increased final rinse temperature for an extended time.

With the Bosch Home Connect app on your smartphone, review the Easy Start best program recommendations based on your input on the type of dishes and cleaning preference option selected, view the estimated water and energy consumption of each program, or adjust a wash option even after the program has started - speed up program time by up to 50% or activate silence mode.
